Wyhcz Books Project



Wyhcz Books was a project initiated by a person named: Jia xian / Howl', as a Community Github Project to suit bookreaders a variety of fresh content to absorb. The main purpose of this website is to allow customers to view the different available books that are being sold by our physical stores! Online purchases however are not possible right now as we're just a start-up company, and we do not have a database server to compile user data. Apart from that, you can take your time browsing and reading through the synopsis for the books that may interest you!
